REGISTRATION

The fee of CIFRIS24 is

  1. 190.00 euros, regular fee;
  2. 170.00 euros, fee for members of Unione Matematica Italiana, IEEE Computer Society, IEEE Information Theory Society;
  3. 120.00 euros, young fee (less than 30 year old on 1 January 2025);
  4. 120.00 euros, fee for members of De Cifris (“ordinario”, ”benemerito” or “fondatore”);
  5. 70.00 euros, fee for members of De Cifris “socio giovane” (young members);
  6. 40.00 euros, companion fee.

The fee (except for the companion fee) covers:

  • free entrance to the main conference room and to the workshops rooms;
  • all coffee breaks and all lunches (25, 26 and 25 September);
  • social dinner (26 September);
  • a copy of the “Proceedings";

PS: membership of De Cifris costs 20 euros "regular" and 5 euros "young", but it is reserved to EU citizens.

To register, please send an email to cifris24@decifris.it.
In the email please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please send us its fiscal data).

Registration Deadline September 2024.
