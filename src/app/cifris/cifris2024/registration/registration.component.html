The additional fee for the social dinner is 50 EUR, and covers the dinner of September 25th.

It is possible to join the social dinner with a Companion (Companion fee: 50 EUR), subject to the availability of seats at the venue. Please select the Companion fee option below to pay for a companion, then send an email to cifris24@decifris.it.

PAYMENT

Before paying, please send an email to cifris24@decifris.it to register.
Accepted payment methods are wire transfer or credit card (NEXI, via the form below).

In the email, please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please also attach its fiscal data).
