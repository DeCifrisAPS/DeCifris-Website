REGISTRATION

The fees of CIFRIS24 excluding workshops (i.e., 25 and 26 September, but not 27) and excluding social dinner are

  1. 260.00 euros, regular fee;
  2. 240.00 euros, fee for members of Unione Matematica Italiana, IEEE Computer Society, IEEE Information Theory Society;
  3. 180.00 euros, young fee (less than 30 year old on 1 January 2025);
  4. 130.00 euros, fee for members of De Cifris (“ordinario”, ”benemerito”, “fondatore”, "giovane").

The fee covers:

  • Free entrance to the main conference room;
  • Coffee breaks and lunches (25 and 26 September).

The fees of CIFRIS24 only workshops (i.e., only 27 September) and excluding social dinner are

  1. 140.00 euros, regular fee;
  2. 120.00 euros, fee for members of Unione Matematica Italiana, IEEE Computer Society, IEEE Information Theory Society;
  3. 95.00 euros, young fee (less than 30 year old on 1 January 2025);
  4. 60.00 euros, fee for members of De Cifris (“ordinario”, ”benemerito”, “fondatore”, "giovane")

The fee covers:

  • Free entrance to the workshops' rooms;
  • Lunch at the main conference room (27 September).

The joint fee of CIFRIS24, main event and workshops (25, 26 and 27 September)

  1. 340.00 euros, regular fee;
  2. 300.00 euros, fee for members of Unione Matematica Italiana, IEEE Computer Society, IEEE Information Theory Society;
  3. 220.00 euros, young fee (less than 30 year old on 1 January 2025);
  4. 160.00 euros, fee for members of De Cifris (“ordinario”, ”benemerito”, “fondatore”, "giovane")

The joint fee covers:

  • Free entrance to the main conference room;
  • Free entrance to the workshops' rooms;
  • All coffee breaks and all lunches (25, 26 and 27 September).

Social dinner fee: 50 euros (25 September).

PS: membership of De Cifris costs 20 euros "regular" and 5 euros "young", but it is reserved to EU citizens.

To register, please send an email to cifris24@decifris.it.
In the email please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please send us its fiscal data).
