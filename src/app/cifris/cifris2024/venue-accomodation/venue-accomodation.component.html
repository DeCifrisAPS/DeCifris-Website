THE VENUE

Main conference

The main confernece of CIFRIS24 will take place in Bank of Italy's conference theater "Salone Margherita", located in Via Due Macelli 75 – 00187 Roma (Italy). Salone Margherita was built in 1898 as a café-chantant and became one of the most famous theatres in Rome.

Read more about the location on
  • Wikipedia (IT)
  • Salone Margherita's website (IT)

Workshops

All workshops will take place in the rooms of Cinema Barberini, P.za Barberini, 24/26, 00187 Roma RM.

HOW TO ARRIVE

Salone Margherita, the location of the main conference (top left of the map), can be easily reached from the train terminal Roma Termini (bottom right):
  • Walking. With a 20-minute walk from Roma Termini.
  • Metro. In five minutes with Metro Line A, from the stop Roma Termini to either Barberini or Spagna.
    The line has color orange and you will find a train every ten minutes.
Cinema Barberini is in Piazza Barberini, right beside the metro station.
