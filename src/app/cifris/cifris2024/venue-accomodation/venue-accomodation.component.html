THE VENUE

All the events of CIFRIS24 take place in Bank of Italy's conference theater "Salone Margherita", located in Via Due Macelli 75 – 00187 Roma (Italy). Salone Margherita was built in 1898 as a café-chantant and became one of the most famous theatres in Rome.

Read more about the location on
  • Wikipedia (IT)
  • Salone Margherita's website (IT)

HOW TO ARRIVE

The location (top left) can be easili reached from the train terminal Roma Termini (bottom right):
  • Walking. With a 20-minute walk from Roma Termini.
  • Metro. In five minutes with Metro Line A, from the stop Roma Termini to either Barberini or Spagna.
    The line has color orange and you will find a train every ten minutes.
