Combinatorics 2024 REGISTRATION

Registration closed on the 07/05/2024.

The fee of combinatorics2024 is:

  1. 300.00 euros, full early registration;
  2. 275.00 euros, full early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  3. 250.00 euros, student early registration fee;
    (Fee reserved to PhD students and postdoc researchers who obtained their PhD after June 2022)
  4. 240.00 euros, student early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  5. 350 euros, full late registration fee;
  6. 325.00 euros, full late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  7. 300.00 euros, student late registration fee;
  8. 290.00 euros, student late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  9. 170.00 euros, accompanying person.

Deadlines

  • Early registration payment 24/02/2024
  • Late registration payment 07/05/2024

NB: Students from the Polytechnic University of Bari can attend for free.

Unfortunately, there is a limited number of places due to the hosting structure. Registrations will close when this number is reached or at latest on May 7th 2024.
If you are Italian or another European Union citizen, we strongly encourage you to become a member of De Cifris, since its membership costs only 20 euros or 5 euros (depending on your age), and it gives you a significant discount on the fees. To become a member, just follow these instructions: https://www.decifris.it/associarsi .

To register for Combinatorics 2024, please:

  1. Send an email to combinatorics2024@decifris.it with your name and your affiliation as subject (for example, "Albert Turing, University of Trento").
    In the email please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please send us its fiscal data).
  2. EITHER pay with your bank, following the instruction in your email reply;
    OR pay via credit card filling the form below.
