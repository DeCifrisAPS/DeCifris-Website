Institutional Session

Description

The Institutional Session of CIFRIS24 brings together key stakeholders from the cryptography and cybersecurity sectors. This session provides a unique platform for institutional and industrial leaders to discuss the latest advancements, challenges, and strategic directions in the field. Attendees will gain insights into current trends and future developments, fostering collaboration between academia, industry, and government entities. Through a series of keynote addresses, panel discussions, and networking opportunities, the Institutional Session aims to strengthen the ties between research and practical applications, ensuring the continuous evolution and security of cryptographic practices.
