PROCEEDINGS

Each talk presented at De Cifris workshops will be published as follows:

  • A two-page mini-abstract will appear in the main Proceedings book, which will be an issue of our book series De Cifris Koine. The paper will also appear in other forms freely: since the mini-abstract is only two-page long, its publication in Koine will not affect the publication of the full paper in other venues.

Remarks:

  1. In any case, a two-page abstract must appear in Koine. Authors of accepted workshop talks are expected to send it to editorial@decifris.it not later than the 4-th of September.
  2. Koine is an Open Access publications, with no fees for authors, published by De Cifris Press, our own publishing house.
  3. Koine is a book series that will not be indexed by search engines, such as Scopus. Its books only have the goal to showcase the activities of the Italian community.
