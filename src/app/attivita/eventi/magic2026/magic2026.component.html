MaGIC 2026 - Marche Workshop on Group Actions in Cryptography

magic-workshop.github.io

MaGIC 2026 is a workshop dedicated to the study of cryptographic group actions, a rapidly evolving area at the intersection of algebraic geometry, number theory, and post-quantum cryptography. The workshop will bring together researchers working in isogeny-based and code-based cryptography, spanning theoretical foundations and practical applications of group-action techniques.

The event will take place in the beautiful setting of Borgo Lanciano, a historic venue in the Marche region of Italy, providing an ideal environment for scientific exchange and collaboration.
