Public key category

Rules

This race only includes an attack phase.
On November 14, each team is given the same public keys of asymmetric cryptosystems:

  • At least 10 public key for RSA di cui:
    • At least 7 of these of ascending length from 100 to 500 bits (which can be broken with factorization algorithms)
    • At least 3 of these with 1000 bits (wich are in turn weak keys)
  • at least 3 public keys of a cipher on celliptic curves
  • at least 1 public key of a cipher on hyperelliptic curves
  • at least 1 public key for HFE
  • at least 1 public key for lattices

Each team must try to find the private key of the cryptosystems starting from the public ones using all the means, mathematical and IT (legitimate!) At their disposal.
Attention, CryptoWars are not a Hacking Contest, but a Cryptography contest.
This race ends on March 12, 2011.

Score

Whenever a key is broken, this team earns points (depending on the length of the key and the cryptosystem) and this key is removed from the list of those that can be attacked.
