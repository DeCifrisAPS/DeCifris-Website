Open Access Statement

diamond We are proudly 100% Diamond Open Access

Our commitment to Diamond Open Access ensures that no fees are charged for both authors and readers. This model promotes a more inclusive and equitable access to knowledge by removing financial barriers to publishing and reading academic work.

Diamond Open Access means:

  • No publication fees: Authors can publish their work without paying any processing fees.
  • Free access for all: Readers can access all content without any cost, ensuring widespread dissemination of knowledge.

This reflects our mission to encourage open and global access to scientific research, ensuring that knowledge can be shared and utilized by all.
