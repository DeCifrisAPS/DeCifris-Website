OpeRa2024 REGISTRATION

The fee of OpeRa2024 is:

  1. 100.00 euros, late fee;
  2. 68.00 euros, early-bird fee (until November 30th);
  3. 75.00 euros, late fee for members of De Cifris;
  4. 55.00 euros, early-bird fee for members of De Cifris (until November 30th).

The fee covers:

  • Facilities;
  • Coffee breaks;
  • Social dinner.

Unfortunately, there is a limited number of places, due to the hosting structure. Registrations will close when this number is reached or at latest on December 31st 2023.
If you are Italian or another European Union citizen, we strongly encourage you to become a member of De Cifris, since it costs only 20 euros or 5 euros (depending on your age), and gives you a significant discount on the fees. To become a member, just follow these instructions: https://www.decifris.it/associarsi .

To register for OpeRa2024, please:

  1. Send an email to opera2024@decifris.it with subject "OpeRa2024".
    In the email please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please send us its fiscal data).
  2. EITHER pay with your bank, following the instruction in your email reply;
    OR pay via credit card filling the form below.
