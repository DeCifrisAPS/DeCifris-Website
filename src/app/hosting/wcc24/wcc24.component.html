WCC 2024 REGISTRATION

The fee of WCC 2024 is:

  1. 350.00 euros, full early registration;
  2. 325.00 euros, full early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  3. 300.00 euros, student early registration fee;
    (Fee reserved to PhD students and postdoc researchers who obtained their PhD after June 2022)
  4. 290.00 euros, student early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  5. 400 euros, full late registration fee;
  6. 375.00 euros, full late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  7. 350.00 euros, student late registration fee;
  8. 340.00 euros, student late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  9. 150.00 euros, accompanying person.

The registration and the accompanying person fees include coffee-breaks and lunches for the 5 days of the conference.

Deadlines

  • Early registration payment 01/06/2024
  • Late registration payment 14/06/2024

Unfortunately, there is a limited number of places, due to the hosting structure. Registrations will close when this number is reached or at latest on June 14th 2024.
If you are Italian or another European Union citizen, we strongly encourage you to become a member of De Cifris, since its membership costs only 20 euros or 5 euros (depending on your age), and it gives you a significant discount on the fees. To become a member, just follow these instructions: https://www.decifris.it/associarsi .

To register for WCC 2024, please:

  1. Send an email to wcc2024@decifris.it with your name and your affiliation as subject (for example, "Albert Turing, University of Trento").
    In the email please specify whether you pay yourself or your company/institution pays for you (in that case, please send us its fiscal data).
  2. EITHER pay with your bank, following the instruction in your email reply;
    OR pay via credit card filling the form below.
