combinatorics2024 REGISTRATION

The fee of combinatorics2024 is:

  1. 300.00 euros, full early registration;
  2. 275.00 euros, full early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  3. 250.00 euros, student early registration fee;
    (Fee reserved to PhD students and postdoc researchers who obtained their PhD after June 2022)
  4. 240.00 euros, student early registration fee for De Cifris members;
  5. 350 euros, full late registration fee;
  6. 325.00 euros, full late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  7. 300.00 euros, student late registration fee;
  8. 290.00 euros, student late registration fee for De Cifris members;
  9. 170.00 euros, accompanying person.

NB: Students from the Polytechnic University of Bari can attend for free.

Unfortunately, there is a limited number of places, due to the hosting structure. Registrations will close when this number is reached or at latest on May 7th 2024.
If you are Italian or another European Union citizen, we strongly encourage you to become a member of De Cifris, since it costs only 20 euros or 5 euros (depending on your age), and gives you a significant discount on the fees. To become a member, just follow these instructions: https://www.decifris.it/associarsi .
